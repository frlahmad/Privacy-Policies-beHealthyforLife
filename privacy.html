Privacy Policy - beHealthyforLife

Last updated: December 2nd, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to beHealthyforLife. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and handled by beHealthyforLife.

2. Health Tracking & User Data

beHealthyforLife allows you to track wellness activities such as workouts, hydration, and lifestyle habits.

  • Data You Enter: Any information you manually input (such as workout logs, daily goals, or notes) is used only to support app functionality.
  • Usage Purpose: Your data is used exclusively to personalize your experience and help you track your progress.

3. Local Device Storage

All personal information (including activity logs, preferences, and progress data) is stored directly on your device.

  • No data is uploaded to external servers.
  • We do not keep any cloud backups of your personal information.
  • If the application is uninstalled or app data is cleared, all stored information will be permanently removed from your device and cannot be recovered by us.

4. Text and Interaction Data

Some features of beHealthyforLife may involve text inputs (such as notes or reminders). These inputs are processed locally within the app.

  • No text data is transmitted outside your device.

5. Device Permissions

The beHealthyforLife app may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications – to send reminders and motivational alerts.
  • Storage – to save your app data directly on your device.
  • Internet (Optional) – used only for checking updates or loading informational content, never for uploading personal data.
